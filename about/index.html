About Ryan

Based out of Prince Edward Island, Canada, Ryan Elliot Drew is an active performer, writer, and educator. He is the music specialist at the Montessori School of Charlottetown, a teaching artist with the National Art Centre's Music Alive Program, and he is a classicaly trained percussionist who performs regularly throughout North America.
